Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for an account?
Eezy! Just click the "SIGN ME UP!" shown at the right hand corner, fill out the form and carefully follow the five easy steps on the side of the form. Please note that your account number is the phone number you registered with.
How long does it take to activate my account?
Your account is activated upon registration.
Do you keep my credit card information?
NO, we do not. Your card information goes directly through our payment processor - Stripe. All information is sent to Stripe using 128-bit SSL encryption technology.
Who is Stripe?
Stripe is an American financial services and software company headquartered in San Francisco, California, United States that offers payment processing software and application programming interfaces for e-commerce websites and mobile applications.
What type of software is used on your website to protect my credit card information?
We use SSL 128bit encryption on the site and do not hold user credit card information. Aside from that, the Peezy architecture (developed by Interra Networks Inc) uses best-of-breed technology to secure our network.  
How will I be billed?
Peezy is a prepaid service. You will pay in advance so you will not receive a bill. However, Peezy does offer you the ability to automatically recharge your account using the Eezy Charge feature.
How can I recharge my account?
There are 3 simple ways:
  • Manual Recharge: You just need to set the amount that should be charged when your account falls below a certain value. With Manual Recharge, simply select the desired amount and click - it's that eezy! Just click on the RECHARGE button when you log into the members area.
  • Eezy Charge (Automatic Recharge): Using the Eezy Charge feature, you can automatically recharge your account.
  • Paypal: With Paypal all you need do is select the amount and you would be redirected to the Paypal's payment gateway.
Are there any hidden fees with Peezy?
No, there are no hidden charges or fees whatsoever with with PEEZY.
My credit card is not getting accepted, what is wrong?
Peezy takes security very seriously; as such your credit card transaction will be rejected if information provided does not match that in your billing statement. Also, your bank may decide to decline a transaction if they have reason to be suspicious. Please contact support@peezy.com and we will help you resolve this.
Do you bill in 3 minutes increments like the most phone cards companies?
Absolutely not! We bill you in one minute increments. Your minutes are your actual minutes.
How can I check the rates? Do rates vary based on the time of the day?
Our rates are posted on our website and are consistent no matter what time of the day you decide to call. The rates displayed on the website are always what you pay.
Can I manually recharge my account?
Yes. Simply click on the Top Up Button.
Can I add another credit card?
Yes. Simply click on the Top Up Button and click on the link for "Add Card". OR Simply Click in the "Manage Card Tab" and click on the "Add Card" Button.
Are there any charges for making calls to a cell phone?
Rates may be higher for making calls to a cell phone in some countries. Please click on the Rates tab to see the extra cell phone charge rates.
What is Pin Eezy?
This is a "PIN-less dialing feature". Tired of entering your calling card number before you can make a call? With PIN Eezy, no such thing! How does it work? When you sign up, click on the "EEZY BOOK" TAB and simply register your phone numbers. Every time you call Peezy from any one of your pre-registered PIN Eezy telephone numbers, you will not have to enter your account number, just dial your number. Click here for a feature tour.
What is Dial Eezy?
This is Peezys' speed dial feature. When you call the same international numbers over and over again, dialing their numbers can be a hassle. Let Peezy make it Eezy for you. Store up to 9 of your most frequently dialed numbers on your Dial Eezy directory and you are that much closer to talking with your family and friends. You can call a 20 digit number in 5 seconds or less, when you Dial Eezy with PEEZY. Using Eezy Charge feature, you can automatically recharge your account. You just need to set the amount that should be charged when your account falls below a certain value. Just click on the BILLING button when you log into the members area. Click here for a feature tour.
What is Eezy Redial?
Did your call not go through? Were all circuits busy? Do not hang up! When you hear the prompt, dial "##" and then dial "0#" - Peezy will automatically redial the last number you called.
What is Eezy Charge?
You do not have to worry whether your account balance is sufficient for the next call. Eezy charge will automatically recharge your account! From a convenient list of options, you choose how low your balance should go and the recharge value. It's just simple that way! Click here for a feature tour.
What is Eezy Book?
Eezy Book is your personal phone book. See who you call the most, keep track, update and change of your Dial Eezy numbers and all your Pin Eezy Access Phone numbers. Eezy Book changes how you read your billing and calling history, when you update your entries, it personalizes your book, so you know who you call the most, not just the number.
What is Peezy Plans?
Peezy offers different calling rate plans for you to choose from. When you sign up, the default calling plan is the Standard Peezy Plan which has no associated fees
Alternatively, you can sign up for a Regional Peezy Plan which offers lower international calling rates than the Standard Peezy Plan, for a small monthly, nonrefundable fee.
Visit our Rates page to see if your favorite country falls under one of our Peezy Regional Plans.
What is Peezy Points?
Peezy Points is our customer rewards program where we reward loyal Peezy customers whenever you recharge your account, purchase gift certificates or refer your friends.
You can redeem your Peezy Points for Peezy Gift Certificates and other great items that will be added soon.
The more you use Peezy, the more you are rewarded! It's that eezy!

How can I make a call?
Dial 1-866-MY-PEEZY or 1-973-842-2700, enter your card number and PIN - start calling.
What is my card (account) number?
Your card (account) number is the phone number you supplied during sign-up.
I forgot my password, how do I access my account?
Simply use the "Forgot Password" link at the bottom of the login box. Enter your email address; you will get an email with a link to reset your password.
Can I cancel my Account
We hope that you don't ever have to consider canceling your account with us; we do provide refunds under under certain conditions. If you are inconvenienced in anyway, you may contact our customer service department at support@peezy.com or by calling 1-877-286-2646 and we will be more than happy to help you.
How can I buy a Peezy Gift Certificate?
Sending a Peezy Gift Certificate is easy and fast! After you create your personalized gift certificate, charge it to your Peezy account. Then we'll send it instantly to the lucky recipient via email! Just log into your personal Peezy account, click on the "Gift" icon in your personal Peezy account area and follow these steps to create a Peezy Gift Certificate
  • Fill out the information requested, the email address must be valid
  • Select an amount
  • Charge the certificate to any of the credit cards that are on file and send your gift instantly!
Do I have to have a Peezy account to buy someone a Peezy gift certificate?
Yes, at this time you will have to have an active Peezy account to purchase a gift certificate for anyone.
Who can I buy a gift certificate for?
You may purchase a gift certificate for anyone who makes international calls from the United States or Canada.
I do not have all of the contact information for the person I want to give the Peezy Gift certificate to, what now?
You do not need to have all of your recipient's contact information, just their name and their valid email address they can personalize their account when they redeem the gift certificate.
My friend already has a Peezy account can I still give them a gift certificate?
Absolutely! The Gift certificate would be the perfect gift for anyone who needs to keep in touch with people around the world.
I have received a Peezy Gift Certificate, how can I redeem it?
You will know that you received a Peezy gift certificate when you receive an e-mail from us. Your e-mail will contain an Activation Link. Just click on it to go directly to your Peezy certificate!
You'll be taken to your Personal Peezy Page where you'll see your very own Peezy gift certificate! After a brief registration process, you will receive a Peezy account number that you can use to start making phone calls right away.
I have received a Peezy Gift Certificate, how can I redeem it?
Once you claim your gift certificate, you have created a Peezy account. You can log into your account at any time and click on the recharge tab. You have three options for recharging your account, Automatic recharge, manual recharge or Paypal.
I gave my friend a Peezy Gift Certificate and they have recharged their account, do I qualify for the referral bonus?
Absolutely! Once your recipient uses their Peezy Gift Certificate and recharges their account, you will receive the $3 referral bonus applied to your account.
I purchased a gift certificate for someone and they no longer use that email address, how can they retrieve their activation link?
For any missing links, please contact the Peezy Support Team.
Does the Peezy Gift Certificate expire?
An unredeemed Peezy Gift Certificate expires ninety (90) from the date it was issued. We will send the recipient a friendly email reminder one month prior to the date that the Peezy Gift Certificate expires.
